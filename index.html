About the Challenge

@Tutoriola created a challenge where you and a friend don't check your messages for three days. Then, the person with the most messages wins! For more information on the challenge, click here.

About this Site

This site is created and maintained by @RedGuy7 to help you with the whole process of the challenge. It will find a time for you and your partner to check your messages if you have timezone problems. It will report a live count of you and your partners messages to show who's winning. Finally, you can post your results to the leaderboard! Can you get the most messages?
